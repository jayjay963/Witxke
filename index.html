Deadly Intersections

Why Arizona’s Roads Are More Dangerous Than Ever

Jordan Dungca in English 102
9 min read · April 14, 2026

Every day, countless drivers in Arizona make their way across hazardous intersections, but few pay attention to the potential dangers associated with reckless driving. In recent years, common occurrences have become highly dangerous for many drivers in Arizona. The state now has some of the worst records in the country when it comes to fatal intersection accidents, which is a critical problem that must be addressed immediately (ABC15, 26). Many accidents have been transformed into a matter of life and death, which poses a severe threat to many drivers. Dangerous intersection accidents have become an increasing problem for Arizona due to distracted driving and unsafe roadways, creating an urgent need for action before the situation worsens. This issue is particularly crucial for youth drivers in Arizona, who are more likely to drive recklessly and irresponsibly.

Archer, Garrett. “Arizona Has Highest Rate of Fatal Intersection Crashes in the Country.” ABC15 Arizona, 2 Jan. 2026.

One of the key reasons why accidents occur at intersections in Arizona is due to reckless behavior among drivers. Most drivers do not follow traffic laws, and many tend to drive carelessly by speeding or texting while driving. Cities such as Phoenix are characterized by a lot of traffic, most people become impatient on the road and might try to rush through yellow lights or completely disregard them. Distracted driving is also one of the key factors that contribute to fatal crashes at intersections in Arizona. Because of smartphones, people often get distracted while driving and pay less attention to the road for a brief period. This brief moment could result in an accident since a driver is more vulnerable to being careless when using his or her mobile phone (NHTSA, 24).

• • •

Other factors that contribute to intersection crashes include the design of the systems and their condition. Some Arizona intersections were not originally constructed to accommodate the amount of traffic they currently see. Due to rapid population growth, systems in many areas have lagged behind, leading to poorly designed and poorly maintained intersections. Some may have reduced visibility or complicated designs. Traffic lights in some places may be outdated and cannot regulate the movement of vehicles. Unprotected left turn signals are one of the many intersection features that lead to crashes, due to the inability of drivers being able to accurately respond to oncoming traffic. At times, insufficient signage or dim lighting may increase the dangers of driving through some intersections, especially at night. Data from the Arizona Department of Transportation indicates that Phoenix and other cities have relatively high crash rates in intersection areas (ADOT, 25).

Arizona Department of Transportation. 2024 Motor Vehicle Crash Facts for the State of Arizona. 9 July 2025.

The consequences of fatal intersection crashes have far reaching impacts. The first and most important consequence is the death of individuals involved in the accident. It brings the family of the deceased into a sudden state of mourning, causing lasting emotional distress that may last several years. Those who survive the incident also sustain various injuries, ranging from bone fractures, head injuries, and other forms of physical disabilities. Additionally, many accidents put a strain on public emergency services and health institutions. Medical facilities have to tend to the injured, while the police force and fire department respond to accidents daily. Such accidents bring fear and anxiety to the entire society, particularly among parents. Once our community starts feeling unsafe on the road, it alters their driving behavior and the way they travel. As per recent studies, Arizona is one of the states with the highest incidence of fatal crashes in the United States (ABC15, 26).

• • •

This issue affects many more aspects of life than just individuals and their families. These fatal crashes can result in higher rates, legal problems, and financial problems for all parties involved. Communities may suffer as a result of accidents leading to the closing of roads and traffic delays. These occurrences will not only decrease the level of trust that people have in road safety but also affect it in a negative way. At this rate, Arizona may experience an increase in the number of fatalities, higher statistics, and the lack of road safety, which will make traveling around the city dangerous. Unsafe driving habits such as speeding and distracted driving are the leading cause of crashes across the United States (NHTSA, 24).

National Highway Traffic Safety Administration. “Distracted Driving.” U.S. Department of Transportation, 2024.

This problem should not be acknowledged to discriminate against certain people, but rather apply to everybody who uses the road. This means that not only accident victims but all passengers, pedestrians, and drivers are at risk due to intersection safety concerns. Teenage drivers, particularly those operating their cars in busy areas such as Phoenix, must understand the gravity of this situation. While some may consider themselves immune to any consequences from speeding or using their phones, most do not know how quickly everything can escalate into tragedy.

• • •

Another consequence of intersection crashes is the emotional and psychological damage sustained by witnesses or survivors of these accidents. Those drivers who have been involved in a serious accident either as participants or bystanders can suffer from anxiety and trauma, or even phobia, related to driving. Such consequences will make it hard for them to live with routine activities or travel to places for work every day. The victims' families have to grieve, and this situation might negatively affect their mental condition. Certain streets and intersections might be considered hazardous by the members of local communities, which in turn, can lead to shifts in the existing traffic patterns.

In summary, it is shown that fatal crashes at intersections within the state of Arizona are a serious problem that requires immediate action. This type of accident results from risky driving habits and insufficient road safety. Collisions lead to serious injuries and fatalities. This means that if drivers are not educated on the importance of following traffic laws and if the problem of road safety is not tackled in time, it might escalate into something worse. For this reason, people living in Arizona, particularly young drivers, should be made aware of this problem.
